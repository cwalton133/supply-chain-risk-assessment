# Supply Chain Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Supply Chain Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan focused on the Pharmaceutical Industry. The objective of the project is to identify critical supply chain risks, assess their likelihood and business impact, and propose actionable mitigation strategies to improve supply chain resilience and operational continuity.

The project demonstrates practical skills in:

* Research
* Analytical Thinking
* Risk Management
* Supply Chain Management

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Identify major supply chain risk categories
* Analyze vulnerabilities within the pharmaceutical supply chain
* Assess operational, financial, and reputational impacts
* Evaluate risk likelihood using qualitative assessment
* Recommend actionable mitigation strategies

---

# 🏭 Industry Focus

**Pharmaceutical Supply Chain**

The pharmaceutical industry relies heavily on:

* Global sourcing
* Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs)
* International logistics networks
* Third-party manufacturers
* Strict regulatory compliance

This makes the industry highly vulnerable to disruptions such as geopolitical conflicts, cyberattacks, transportation delays, and supplier failures.

---

# ⚠️ Supply Chain Risk Categories Identified

| Risk Category           | Description                                                    |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Geopolitical Risks      | Trade restrictions, wars, sanctions, and political instability |
| Natural Disaster Risks  | Floods, earthquakes, pandemics, and extreme weather            |
| Supplier Failure Risks  | Supplier bankruptcy, shortages, or quality failures            |
| Demand Volatility Risks | Sudden spikes or drops in customer demand                      |
| Cybersecurity Risks     | Ransomware, system breaches, and data attacks                  |

---

# 🔍 High-Impact Risks Assessed

## 1. Supplier Dependency on Foreign API Manufacturers

### Impact

* High Financial Impact
* High Operational Impact

### Likelihood

High

### Mitigation Strategies

* Multi-supplier sourcing strategy
* Increase local sourcing
* Maintain safety stock inventory

---

## 2. Cybersecurity Attacks on Supply Chain Systems

### Impact

* High Operational Impact
* High Reputational Impact

### Likelihood

Medium to High

### Mitigation Strategies

* Multi-factor authentication
* Regular cybersecurity audits
* Incident response planning

---

## 3. Transportation and Logistics Disruptions

### Impact

* High Customer Service Impact
* High Operational Impact

### Likelihood

High

### Mitigation Strategies

* Diversified logistics providers
* Regional distribution centers
* Real-time supply chain visibility systems
